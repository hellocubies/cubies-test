Welcome to CUBIES WORLD!

Create your own Cubie Collection

Been on this website for a few minutes but still unsure about how to navigate it? Don’t worry, this guide will help answer your questions!

GET CUBIES

In the “Get Cubies” section, there’s a button called “Get My Lucky Cubie”. Once you click it, a file of a Cubie will pop up, and you’ll be able to download that file and print it out to make the Cubie. This section refreshes every two hours, so you’ll be able to get a new Cubie then. But beware, it’s possible to get a duplicate Cubie!

CHECKLIST

The “Checklist” section of the website has all the Cubies up to date. Once you get a Cubie, you can press the “I own this Cubie” button in order to confirm that you have to Cubie and to see that Cubie’s origin story. Click the image to enlarge it and click it again to return.

Cubies.

What are they, you ask?

Well, as you can see from our website, they are tiny cubes with cute designs on them, from animals to food. In a word, they are Cubies.

Before they were Cubies, they were ideas and drawings by Cephalopod and his friends when they were in fourth grade. They hand drew them to create them until they realized that this method was extremely time-consuming. Just as soon as they realized this, their interests changed, and Cephalopod stopped making them.

Now skip to a couple of years later. Cephalopod took interest in making these again, but’s time digitally. He really got into them, recruiting LilyFlower and RaiderX to help him out. They created many more of these cute crafts, naming them Cubies. From there, they decided to post them so that others could enjoy them too. And here they are. A website with all Cubies known to humans enjoyed by many others in the world.

Cephalopod

The original Cubie maker, he is known for his originality and creativity. He first started making Cubies with his friends in fourth grade for fun. He quit soon after though as he lost interest. He picked up this idea again a couple of years later, though, and turned it into the huge thing it is today.

RaiderX

The first one to be recruited by Cephalopod to work on the Cubies, RaiderX is known for his dexterity and love of Cubies. The first one to make every single Cubie, he checks to make sure that these Cubies are ready to be published on the website.

LilyFlower

The second and the last one to be recruited, LilyFlower is known for her creativity and her love for books. As a talented writer, she does all of the writing for this website, including the origin stories of the Cubies.
