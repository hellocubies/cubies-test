Cubies.

What are they, you ask?

Well, as you can see from our website, they are tiny cubes with cute designs on them, from animals to food. In a word, they are Cubies.

Before they were Cubies, they were ideas and drawings by Cephalopod and his friends when they were in fourth grade. They hand drew them to create them until they realized that this method was extremely time-consuming. Just as soon as they realized this, their interests changed, and Cephalopod stopped making them.
